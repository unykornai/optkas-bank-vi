## ARTICLE {{ article_number | default("VIII") }} — LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

### Section {{ article_number | default("VIII") }}.3 — Exceptions

The limitations set forth in Sections {{ article_number | default("VIII") }}.1 and {{ article_number | default("VIII") }}.2 shall not apply to:

(a) Fraud or willful misconduct;

(b) Breach of the AML or sanctions representations and warranties set forth herein;

(c) Indemnification obligations for third-party claims; or

(d) Breach of confidentiality obligations.

The indemnification provisions of this Agreement shall be the sole and exclusive remedy of the Parties for any breach of the representations, warranties, covenants, and agreements contained herein, except for claims arising from fraud, which shall not be subject to limitation.
